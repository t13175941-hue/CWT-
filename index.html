Rules & Regulations

Official Digital Rulebook

Looking to review the complete tournament structure clauses, structural parameters, or specific tiebreaker criteria setups?

Open Rulebook

• Every normal league match will strictly consist of **6 overs** per innings.

• A maximum bowling constraint applies: **No single bowler can deliver more than 2 overs** in an innings.

• To ensure seamless schedule tracking, innings must be completed inside **20 minutes**. Slow over rates are subject to immediate run penalties.

• The entire tournament will be strictly conducted using official **Vicky Tennis Balls**.

• Teams are completely responsible for carrying their own playing equipment, specifically cricket bats. Match-ready Vicky balls will be continuously supplied by the on-ground organizing body.

• The tournament completely locks registration at **48 active players maximum** across **6 teams total**.

• Every individual squad represents **7 active field players** and exactly **1 designated substitution player** (8 total squad slots).

• Absolutely no outside wildcard player entries are permitted once match schedules commence. Substitute fielders are authorized under strict physical injury assessment, entirely subject to the central umpire's green light.

• The 3 outright winners from the initial qualifier matches advance directly. The top-performing losing team completes the 4-team playoff field via the wildcard slot.

• All 4 qualifying teams are sorted globally by Net Run Rate (NRR) and matched into distinct brackets:

• Semifinal 1: Rank #1 NRR Team vs Rank #2 NRR Team

• Semifinal 2: Rank #3 NRR Team vs Rank #4 NRR Team

NRR Math Formulation: [Total Runs Scored / Total Overs Faced] - [Total Runs Conceded / Total Overs Bowled]

• **Critical Caveat:** If a batting team loses all 7 wickets before batting out their assigned quota, the calculator treats the inning as completing the full assigned quota structurally (**6.0 overs**).

• The field umpire's decision is completely absolute, final, and binding under all circumstances.

• Any physical/verbal altercation or behavior undermining match officials triggers negative deduction penalties directly affecting your standings on the **Best Discipline Leaderboard** and carries risks of total team disqualification.
